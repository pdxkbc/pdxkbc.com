The Portland Keyboard Club (PDXKBC) is a group for people of all ages and skill levels to showcase and discuss mechanical keyboards.

COVID-19

For the forseeable future, PDXKBC will be an online-only group. Our Discord chat is more active than ever, and we'd love to chat about keyboards with you.

Let's Talk

You can chat with us on Discord. Please read the Code of Conduct before joining our server.
