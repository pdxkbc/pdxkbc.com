Portland Keyboard Club

The Portland Keyboard Club (PDXKBC) is a monthly meetup group for people of all ages and skill levels to showcase and discuss mechanical keyboards.

Meetups are on the last Saturday of each month, from 6:00–9:00 PM, with a prize giveaway at 7:30 PM. See our Google Calendar for more event details.

You can chat with us on Discord or check our Reddit for meetup info.

Our official hashtag is #pdxkbc.

If you have any questions or concerns, please email the organizer, Brian Mock, at brian@mockbrian.com, or join the Discord.
