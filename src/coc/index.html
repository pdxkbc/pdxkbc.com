Code of Conduct

All members of the PDXKBC agree to the following Code of Conduct.

  • No sexual, violent, or offensive content.

  • No harassment or discrimination based on gender, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion, etc.

  • GIFs and animated emojis should not flash, change, color, or animate excessively (this can cause seizures or otherwise be a nuisance).

Anyone violating these rules may be temporarily or permanently banned at the discretion of the admins, depending on the severity of the violation, or if behavior is repeated despite warnings.
